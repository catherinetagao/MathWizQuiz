Subtracting Tens and Ones

Objectives:

• Learn how to subtract tens and ones.
• Understand subtraction without regrouping.

Examples:

1. Subtracting Numbers:
20 - 10: 10
45 - 20: 25
50 - 30: 20
90 - 50: 40
80 - 60: 20

Steps to Subtract:

1. Line up the numbers by place value.
2. Subtract the ones place.
3. Subtract the tens place.
4. Write down the result.
