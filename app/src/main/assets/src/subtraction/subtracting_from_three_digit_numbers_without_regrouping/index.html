Subtracting from 3-digit Numbers without Regrouping

Objectives:

• Learn how to subtract from 3-digit numbers without regrouping.
• Understand basic subtraction concepts.

Examples:

1. Subtracting Numbers:
123 - 100: 23
456 - 200: 256
789 - 300: 489
987 - 400: 587
654 - 500: 154

Steps to Subtract:

1. Line up the numbers by place value.
2. Subtract the ones place.
3. Subtract the tens place.
4. Subtract the hundreds place.
5. Write down the result.
