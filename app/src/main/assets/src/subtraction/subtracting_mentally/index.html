Subtracting Mentally

Objectives:

• Learn how to subtract numbers mentally.
• Improve mental math skills.
• Practice mental subtraction with various problems.

Examples:

1. Subtracting Simple Numbers:
50 - 25: 25
100 - 45: 55
200 - 123: 77
75 - 33: 42
90 - 67: 23

Steps to Subtract Mentally:

1. Break the numbers into parts that are easier to subtract.
2. Subtract the parts separately.
3. Combine the results.
4. Double-check your answer.
