Subtracting from 3-digit Numbers with Regrouping, with or without Zero Difficulty

Objectives:

• Learn how to subtract from 3-digit numbers with regrouping.
• Understand the concept of borrowing in subtraction, including handling zeros in the minuend.

Examples:

1. Subtracting Numbers:
512 - 289: 223
705 - 467: 238
300 - 189: 111
813 - 572: 241
401 - 364: 37

Steps to Subtract:

1. Line up the numbers by place value.
2. If the digit in the subtrahend is larger than the digit in the minuend, borrow 1 from the next column.
3. If borrowing from a zero, continue borrowing from the next column.
4. Subtract the ones place.
5. Subtract the tens place.
6. Subtract the hundreds place.
7. Write down the result.
