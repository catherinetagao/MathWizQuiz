Subtracting from 3-digit Numbers with Regrouping

Objectives:

• Learn how to subtract from 3-digit numbers with regrouping.
• Understand the concept of borrowing in subtraction.

Examples:

1. Subtracting Numbers:
512 - 289: 223
745 - 476: 269
694 - 238: 456
913 - 572: 341
821 - 364: 457

Steps to Subtract:

1. Line up the numbers by place value.
2. If the digit in the subtrahend is larger than the digit in the minuend, borrow 1 from the next column.
3. Subtract the ones place.
4. Subtract the tens place.
5. Subtract the hundreds place.
6. Write down the result.
