Subtracting Money in Pesos

Objectives:

• Learn how to subtract money values in pesos.
• Understand the importance of aligning decimal points when subtracting.
• Practice subtracting different amounts of money.

Examples:

1. Subtracting Money Values:
₱575.00 - ₱250.00: ₱325.00
₱1000.00 - ₱475.00: ₱525.00
₱2000.00 - ₱1535.00: ₱465.00
₱5050.00 - ₱2525.00: ₱2525.00
₱10000.00 - ₱6540.00: ₱3460.00

Steps to Subtract:

1. Write down the money amounts, aligning the decimal points.
2. If the cents in the subtrahend are larger than in the minuend, borrow from the pesos.
3. Subtract the cents.
4. Subtract the pesos.
5. Write down the result.
