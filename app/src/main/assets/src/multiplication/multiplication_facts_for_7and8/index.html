Multiplication Facts for 7 and 8

Objectives:

• Understand the concept of multiplication by 7 and 8.
• Learn and memorize the multiplication facts for 7 and 8.
• Practice solving multiplication problems involving the numbers 7 and 8.

Examples:

Multiplication Facts for 7:
1. 7 x 1 = 7
2. 7 x 2 = 14
3. 7 x 3 = 21
4. 7 x 4 = 28
5. 7 x 5 = 35
6. 7 x 6 = 42
7. 7 x 7 = 49
8. 7 x 8 = 56
9. 7 x 9 = 63
10. 7 x 10 = 70

Multiplication Facts for 8:
1. 8 x 1 = 8
2. 8 x 2 = 16
3. 8 x 3 = 24
4. 8 x 4 = 32
5. 8 x 5 = 40
6. 8 x 6 = 48
7. 8 x 7 = 56
8. 8 x 8 = 64
9. 8 x 9 = 72
10. 8 x 10 = 80

Steps to Learn Multiplication by 7 and 8:

1. Understand that multiplying by 7 is the same as adding a number seven times and multiplying by 8 is the same as adding a number eight times.
2. Memorize the basic multiplication facts for 7 and 8 (7 x 1 through 7 x 10 and 8 x 1 through 8 x 10).
3. Use repetition and practice to reinforce your memory.
4. Apply the facts in real-life situations to better understand their use.
5. Practice with interactive problems to test your knowledge.
