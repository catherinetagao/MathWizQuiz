Multiplication Facts for 4

Objectives:

• Understand the concept of multiplication by 4.
• Learn and memorize the multiplication facts for 4.
• Practice solving multiplication problems involving the number 4.

Examples:

1. 4 x 1 = 4
2. 4 x 2 = 8
3. 4 x 3 = 12
4. 4 x 4 = 16
5. 4 x 5 = 20
6. 4 x 6 = 24
7. 4 x 7 = 28
8. 4 x 8 = 32
9. 4 x 9 = 36
10. 4 x 10 = 40

Steps to Learn Multiplication by 4:

1. Understand that multiplying by 4 is the same as adding a number four times.
2. Memorize the basic multiplication facts for 4 (4 x 1 through 4 x 10).
3. Use repetition and practice to reinforce your memory.
4. Apply the facts in real-life situations to better understand their use.
5. Practice with interactive problems to test your knowledge.
